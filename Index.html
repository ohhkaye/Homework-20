I CARE: a UX Case Study

As the internet evolves, having a strong online presence and an intuitive UI is becoming increasingly important to remain relevant. My role as a UX Designer and Researcher was to refresh the UI of the 501(c)(3) organization I CARE to help it reach more volunteers to achieve its mission of helping low-income seniors get rides to the places they need to go.

Context

The Problem

People interested in helping seniors within their community struggle to understand how to volunteer with I CARE due to a lack of information about responsibilities, requirements, and scheduling.

The Hypothesis

In reviewing the I CARE organization's website, we believe that (1) providing clear and accessible information via intuitive information architecture and (2) designing a volunteer portal will enable users to understand volunteer requirements and successfully participate in the program.

The User

To best understand our user, we came up with Jenn, our proto-persona, as a woman of the "sandwich generation" who is well accustomed to caring for others and feels a deep sense of responsibility when it comes to caring for the older generation and giving back to her local community.

Research

We started by interviewing people to find out their interests, motivators, and pain points when it comes to volunteering.

Empathy Map

Having connected with a number of potential volunteers, we found we understood our user better.

- She SAYS she wants to make a lasting impact on her community.

- She THINKS volunteering is a worthwhile activity.

- She FEELS connected to others through volunteering and wants to make meaningful relationships.

- She WANTS to volunteer locally.

Findings

Insight

From interviewing potential volunteers, we learned that volunteers have a desire to make a positive impact in their community but may face barriers participating with the I CARE organization due to unclear volunteer requirements, missing vehicle specifications, and no insight into the process of scheduling a shift.

Value

Operating as a 501c non-profit organization, I CARE offers safe and reliable transportation services to senior citizens and gives interested volunteers the opportunity to make a meaningful difference in their community.
